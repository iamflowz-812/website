About FLOWZ

FLORIAN AKA FLOWZ

Drum & Bass artist from Cologne, Germany. His connection to music started early through piano and later turned into a deep passion for electronic music and DJ culture.

What began with internet radio stations and a first DJ controller at the age of 13 evolved into years of practice, club experience and a relentless commitment to growth. Today, FLOWZ is known for energetic, story-driven sets that move between Dancefloor, Neurofunk and Jump Up.

From warm-up to peak time or closing, he adapts confidently to every slot – always aiming to create high-energy moments, emotional turns and unforgettable crowd reactions.

"In every set, I try to tell a story and take people on an emotional journey."
